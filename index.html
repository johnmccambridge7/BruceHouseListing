About Us

We're a beautiful seaside 4 bedroom bungalow with a stunning shore side location and sublime views over the sea toward the Isle of Skye. 'Beau Vallon' offers its guests a unique and comfortable base to explore the delights of the west coast of Scotland. Sleeping a maximum of 6 people this holiday home is a detached dwelling nestled in the wonderful highland village of Glenelg, Scotland.
